Welcome to the HR Dashboard!

Welcome, team, to our new HR Dashboard! This dashboard is designed to provide you with a comprehensive overview of our human resources metrics and insights. Here's what you can expect:

Key Features:

  • Employee Demographics: Visualizations and breakdowns of employee age, gender, department, and tenure.
  • Recruitment Insights: Track recruitment metrics such as time-to-hire, source of hire, and open positions.
  • Performance Metrics: Overview of performance reviews, employee ratings, and goal achievements.
  • Attendance and Leave: Monitor attendance trends, leave balances, and absenteeism rates.
  • Employee Satisfaction: Feedback from employee surveys and engagement scores.

Getting Started:

  • Explore the Dashboard: Navigate through different sections to get a feel of the available data.
  • Interactive Filters: Use the filters to drill down into specific departments, locations, or time periods.
  • Regular Updates: The data will be updated regularly to ensure you have the latest information.

Purpose of This Dashboard:

This dashboard aims to enhance our understanding of the workforce and support data-driven decisions. Whether you're in HR, management, or any other department, you'll find valuable insights to help improve our work environment and operational efficiency.


Feel free to reach out with any questions or feedback. Happy exploring!
